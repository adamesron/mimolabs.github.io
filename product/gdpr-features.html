GDPR Compliance

From May 25th '18, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) will be in place.

We've worked hard here at MIMO to ensure that you have all the tools you need to remain GDPR compliant.

If you have any concerns or questions, get in touch!

GDPR Friendly Splash Pages

Even if you're not using the built-in email capture, you're capturing personal data in the form of IP & MAC addresses. The GDPR says that you must obtain explicit, opt-in consent, and be clear about how your customer data will be used.

  • GDPR friendly splash pages that are fast to set-up. You'll be able to get (and record) your user's consent.
  • Automatically add a checkbox so your users can decide what to sign-up for.
  • Control SMS & Email notification settings separately.
  • Automatically control the cookie & privacy policy.
  • Control the text and add your own terms.
  • Disable access completely if they don't provide consent or opt-out.

Customer Timeline

You also need to give your users a facility to view, download and delete their profile data.

We’ve built you a whole new section so you can search and browse through your customers. We’re calling them — timelines!

When the user agrees to the terms, the action is now logged into their timeline so you and they can see when they agreed and what they agreed to, as well as tracking all of your messages to them.

Data Request Form

Your customers can now request their data from the splash pages/MIMO website.

They just need to enter their email address at the below url, and they will be sent a link.

oh-mimo.com/gdpr-wifi-tools/request

They’ll see their customer timeline and will be able to delete and download all their data. High five.

Double Opt-In Email Capture

To keep you GDPR compliant when you use internal integration with your Email login type, your guests will now be sent an email upon signing in to confirm that they agree to their email being used for newsletters/marketing purposes.

Their email will not show in your lists until they confirm this.
