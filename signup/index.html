Things You Should Know

You charge for people, what are people?

Everyone with a unique ID in your MIMO account counts towards the total number of people in your account at any one time.

What if a person has multiple devices?

Where possible, we'll bundle them together under one ID so you only pay for a single person. You can also manually merge people too.

What happens when I delete some people?

If you delete some people, you'll be billed less next month assuming you've dropped into a lower tier. You don't need to do anything else.

Can I try both plans during the 14 day trial?

Sure you can. We recommend you try the Engage Plan first because it's pretty freaking awesome.

What do you mean by emails?

Emails are outbound messages that are sent when events occur. You can create campaigns that send your messages when your customer first connects or if they don't come back for a while.

How many emails can I send?

On the Engage Plan, you get 100,000 emails included every month. That's a lot. If you go over, you can hop onto the Engage Plus Plan. It's unlikely you'll go over though.

Do you offer discounts?

No we don't, everyone gets the same great deal.

How many access points can I add?

As many as you freaking want. There are no additional costs for APs.
