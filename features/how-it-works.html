What is MIMO?

MIMO allows you to easily offer awesome WiFi experiences to your customers. Create beautiful splash pages to show off your brand, gain valuable insight into your customers, and take action with all the data you have.
Minimum Input, Maximum Output.

Integrations

We've put in the hard work so you can quickly and easily integrate with your favourite access points and vendors.

Setup only takes a few minutes, and no extra hardware or software is needed!

As an added bonus, you can also manage your networks if your access points are running CT firmware.
